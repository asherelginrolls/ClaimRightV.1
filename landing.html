The process

Three steps. Five minutes. No lawyer.

ClaimRight reads the rejection letter, maps it against the regulations insurers hope you never read, and gives you a pack that's ready to file with the Insurance Ombudsman. You stay in control the whole way.

Step 01

Upload the rejection letter and your policy.

PDFs, WhatsApp screenshots, scanned photos — whatever your insurer sent. We pull out the claim number, the reason code, the dates, and the exact clause the insurer cited.

Step 02

We map your case to the IRDAI rules and ombudsman rulings that apply.

Master Circular §6.2. PPOI moratorium clock. The 1-hour cashless deadline. The 2% interest clause on delayed settlements. We cite every argument to the exact regulation and to real ombudsman awards on similar cases.

Step 03

You get a dispute pack, ready to file.

Drafted letter in your words. Citation index. Escalation timeline. Everything you need to file with GRO, Bima Bharosa, or the Ombudsman. Track the response clock inside the app.
